Pipeline status

✅ Geometry model — solid-of-revolution profile, exact mass/inertia/CG, live PDGA legality (disc_model.py, designer app, Grasshopper)
✅ Data tier — full PDGA certified-disc database; pro-disc presets derived from certified specs
✅ Calibrated flight numbers — app estimate fit to 43 pro discs' manufacturer ratings, LOO-validated (scripts/fit_flight_numbers.py)
✅ Flight sim (Tier 2) — 6-DOF dynamics with Hummel coefficients, calibrated to the disc-golf regime (flight_sim.py)
✅ CFD sweep — 96 geometries × 7 angles of steady RANS (k-ω SST), 669 converged solves (cfd/bigsweep/)
✅ Surrogate model — degree-2 polynomial fit of the sweep drives the designer's flight path and the wind-tunnel charts (cfd_surrogate.js)
⏳ Spinning CFD — add rotation (MRF) to the RANS runs to capture spin-dependent effects
