PDGA certified-disc database
Official measurements from the PDGA certified-disc database (snapshot July 2026, data/pdga_approved_discs.csv). Regenerate with scripts/build_disc_db.py. Click a column to sort; click a row to open it in the designer as a starting shape.

Throw a disc: CFD-surrogate aerodynamics, 6-DOF integration

Aerodynamic coefficients come from the CFD surrogate evaluated at the selected disc's geometry, anchored to Kamaruddin's wind-tunnel measurements of real discs (cfd/anchors/); the integrator adds gravity, spin, and gyroscopic precession (cfd_flight.js, the same physics as flight_sim.py). Positive hyzer tilts the outer edge down at release; nose angle pitches the nose up. Pin a trajectory, then change disc or throw to compare.

Python reference: four presets, intermediate arm speeds

Produced by flight_plots.py from the 6-DOF simulator. Lift-supported glide, gyroscopic precession, and low-speed fade emerge from the equations rather than being scripted.

Putter cross-section: geometry model output

From disc_model.py: the same solid-of-revolution profile the designer app and the Grasshopper component use.

Run it yourself

python3 flight_sim.py prints a single trajectory with touchdown stats. python3 flight_plots.py regenerates the preset comparison figure. Coefficients and caveats are in the Methods doc.

Pipeline status

✅ Geometry model: solid-of-revolution profile, exact mass/inertia/CG, live PDGA legality (disc_model.py, designer app, Grasshopper)
✅ Data tier: full PDGA certified-disc database; pro-disc presets derived from certified specs
✅ Flight sim: 6-DOF dynamics, validated against Kamaruddin's published golf-disc flights: putter/mid ranges within ±3% under matched assumptions (flight_sim.py, cfd/verify_trajectory.py)
✅ CFD sweep: 96 geometries × 7 angles of steady RANS (k-ω SST), 669 converged solves; expansion to 300 geometries in progress (cfd/bigsweep/)
✅ Surrogate model: degree-2 polynomial fit of the sweep, anchored to Kamaruddin's wind-tunnel level via three measured discs (lift ×0.85, drag ×0.98); drives the designer's flight path and the wind-tunnel charts (cfd_surrogate.js, cfd/anchors/)
✅ CFD flight numbers: speed/glide/turn/fade computed from the surrogate's aero coefficients, calibrated to 43 pro discs' ratings, LOO-validated (scripts/fit_cfd_flight_numbers.py; geometry-fit fallback: scripts/fit_flight_numbers.py)
⏳ Spinning CFD: add rotation (MRF) to the RANS runs to capture spin-dependent effects
