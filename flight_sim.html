Throw a disc: CFD-surrogate aerodynamics, 6-DOF integration

Aerodynamic coefficients come from the CFD surrogate evaluated at the selected disc's geometry, anchored to Kamaruddin's wind-tunnel measurements of real discs (cfd/anchors/); the integrator adds gravity, spin, and gyroscopic precession (cfd_flight.js, the same physics as flight_sim.py). Tilt with the edge down is a hyzer release and edge up is anhyzer; nose angle pitches the nose up. Pin a trajectory, then change disc or throw to compare.

Python reference: four presets, intermediate arm speeds

Produced by flight_plots.py from the 6-DOF simulator. Lift-supported glide, gyroscopic precession, and low-speed fade emerge from the equations rather than being scripted.

Run it yourself

python3 flight_sim.py prints a single trajectory with touchdown stats. python3 flight_plots.py regenerates the preset comparison figure. Coefficients and caveats are in the Methods doc.
