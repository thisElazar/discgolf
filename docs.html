Pipeline status

✅ Geometry model: solid-of-revolution profile, exact mass/inertia/CG, live PDGA legality (disc_model.py, designer app, Grasshopper)
✅ Data tier: full PDGA certified-disc database; pro-disc presets derived from certified specs
✅ Flight sim: 6-DOF dynamics, validated against Kamaruddin's published golf-disc flights: putter/mid ranges within ±3% under matched assumptions (flight_sim.py, cfd/verify_trajectory.py)
✅ CFD sweep: 300 geometries × 7 angles of steady RANS (k-ω SST), 2,086 converged solves (cfd/bigsweep/)
✅ Surrogate model: degree-2 polynomial fit of the sweep, anchored to Kamaruddin's wind-tunnel level via three measured discs (lift ×0.85, drag ×0.98); drives the designer's flight path and the wind-tunnel charts (cfd_surrogate.js, cfd/anchors/)
✅ CFD flight numbers: speed/glide/turn/fade computed from the surrogate's aero coefficients, calibrated to 43 pro discs' ratings, LOO-validated (scripts/fit_cfd_flight_numbers.py; geometry-fit fallback: scripts/fit_flight_numbers.py)
⏳ Spinning CFD: add rotation (MRF) to the RANS runs to capture spin-dependent effects
